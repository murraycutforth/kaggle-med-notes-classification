<B>HISTORY: </B> Patient, 37, desires a vasectomy.

<B>PROCEDURE: </B> Through a midline scrotal incision, the right vas was identified and separated from the surrounding tissues, clamped, transected, and tied off with a 4-0 chromic.  No bleeding was identified.
